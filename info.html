Hello, I’m Pietro Vitali, I was born in Modena in 1997 but I'm currently working in Milan. I graduated in Communication Design at the Politecnico di Milano in 2019. Thanks to my academic studies, I was able to deepen and improve several aspects of my current design, such as Photography, Fashion, Editorial Design, and Architecture. During my studies, I was able to convey those interests through my personal, professional, and academic projects.
This portfolio is structured in order to recognize my general projects’ attitude, presenting eight works I’ve developed personally or in teamwork.
I consider my working approach to be based on strong research with the aim of exploring design transversally into different media, visual languages and outputs.
