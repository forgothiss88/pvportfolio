Hello, I’m Pietro Vitali, I was born in Modena (Italy) in 1997. I hold a Bachelor degree in Communication Design from the Politecnico di Milano, class of 2019, and I am currently concluding a Master’s degree in Visual Communication at the FHNW in Basel. Through my academic studies, I've been able to deepen and improve several aspects of my current design, namely Photography, Fashion, Editorial Design, and Architecture. I am presently highly interested in Visual Research, meaning researching through visual media the potentiality of images, specifically how and why they can convey messages. This portfolio is structured to recognize my general projects’ approach, presenting nine works I’ve developed personally or in teamwork. I tend to work with a strong Research Approach, combining Artistic Research and Design Thinking. Creative direction and coordination are two main parts of my working method. Due to academic studies and personal inclinations, I do research mainly on Visual Images, Gender Studies, Preservation, Conservation, and Curatorship, which are trasversally presented throughout the works presented in this portfolio.
