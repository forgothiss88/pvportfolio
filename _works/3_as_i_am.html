--- title: As I am (2021) slug: as-i-am keywords: - 2021 - 2-week workshop - music documentary - moving images, creative direction, poetry meta_title: "As I am - Pietro Vitali" meta_description: "“As I Am” is an intimate portrayal of poet Carlos Valencia, where poems and images resonate together to create an emotional space for the viewer to witness glimpses of the protagonist’s life." short_desc: "“As I Am” is an intimate portrayal of poet Carlos Valencia, where poems and images resonate together to create an emotional space for the viewer to witness glimpses of the protagonist’s life." imgdir: "/works/as_i_am" img_top_right: "/1.jpg" img_bot_left_1: "/2.jpg" img_bot_left_2: "/3.jpg" img_bot_right: "/5.jpg" img_thumbnail: "/4.jpg" --- “As I Am” is a short movie shot in Neuchâtel (CH) in 2021. The documentary music video follows the leading character Carlos Valencia performing his poems and proses. The introspective content is delivered by the actor's charisma and by the emotional depth of the photography, which vividly portrays the protagonist’s life through his own words. The project lasted two weeks since it was carried out during a summer workshop, with two days of shooting. The outcome results profound and sincere, and yet it sparks joy and hope. The poems and proses are original texts by Carlos Valencia.
