The process considered analyzing the exhibitions, archiving their material and immaterial knowledge, organizing the design reflecting on both the material and immaterial contents. As result, Art Spaces consists of two different format leaflets- representing the primary and secondary content of each exhibition - that can be connected among themselves using diagonal folding methods.
