--- title: As I am title_short: As I am slug: as-i-am keywords: - 2021 - 2-week workshop - music documentary - moving images, creative direction, poetry meta_title: "As I am - Pietro Vitali" meta_description: "2021 - “As I Am” is an intimate portrayal of poet Carlos Valencia, where poems and images resonate together to create an emotional space for the viewer to witness glimpses of the protagonist’s life." tagline: "2021 - video direction" short_desc: "“As I Am” is an intimate portrayal of poet Carlos Valencia, where poems and images resonate together to create an emotional space for the viewer to witness glimpses of the protagonist’s life." imgdir: "/works/as_i_am" img_top_right: "https://i.imgur.com/MTD81dSh.jpg" img_bot_left_1: "https://i.imgur.com/v0LMZlth.png" img_bot_left_2: "https://i.imgur.com/Rlry43ih.jpg" img_bot_right: "https://i.imgur.com/QmdpyfU.mp4" img_thumbnail: "https://i.imgur.com/ZzlGsZ1h.jpg" --- "As I Am" is a short movie that was selected as part of the 47th Schweizer Jugendfilmtage and screened in Zurich on March 24th, 2022. Shot in Neuchâtel (CH) in 2021, this documentary music video follows the leading character Carlos Valencia as he performs his original poems and prose. Despite being shot over just two days during a summer workshop, the introspective content is delivered through the actor's charisma and the emotional depth of the photography, which vividly portrays the protagonist's life through his own words. The outcome is profound and sincere, yet it also sparks joy and hope. The poems and proses are original texts by Carlos Valencia.
